Splunk MINT APP
========================

OVERVIEW
--------
The Splunk MINT App gives you the power of Splunk to gain operational intelligence around your mobile environment. 
The Splunk MINT App aggregates data from the individual mobile app projects you set up in Splunk MINT Management Console,
then lets you view data for any combination of mobile appsâ€”for example, all of your apps for a particular platform, 
or all of your apps that have been released, over any time range.

* Author - Splunk
* Version - 3.0.2
* Compatible with:
  * Splunk Enterprise version: 7.2.x, 7.3.x, 8.0.x
  * OS: Platform independent
